Brownies

Description

Brownies made with cocoa powder, adjustable baking time for desired consistency.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Cocoa powder
  • Water

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients, preheat oven, and prepare baking dish.
  2. Combine dry ingredients in a large bowl.
  3. Mix water, oil, and vanilla into the batter, then spread it evenly.
  4. Bake until top is set, about 30 minutes.
  5. Cool for 10 minutes, then cut into 15 squares.
