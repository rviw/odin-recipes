Waffles

Description

Waffles are delicious and enjoyed by all.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Flour
  • Baking powder

Steps

  1. Preheat a waffle iron.
  2. Mix water and flax seed meal in a bowl.
  3. Blend oats, flax seed mixture, soy milk, flours, oil, baking powder, vanilla, and salt until just mixed.
  4. Cook waffles in preheated iron until golden and crisp.
